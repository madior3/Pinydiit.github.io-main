Who We Are

PINYDIIT FOUNDATION, INC- (pronouns as "Penydiet or Peny-hee-diet") is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization founded in January 2023 with the mission of providing school stationeries and scholarships to underserved students of internally displaced persons (IDPs) and refugee communities of South Sudanese origin within South Sudan and other East African countries. The foundation was named after the Awulian community's village in Nyuak Payam, Twic East County of Jonglei state, South Sudan. According to oral traditional history passed down on many generations, as time goes on, the Pinydiit village of Awulian became "Penydiet," a pronunciation deviation from the word "Pinydiit." Geographically, the village is located south of Bai Pawuoi, another Awulian village, and west of Pawuoi Wut. Our stories aim to inspire and motivate our supporters to continue their support for our education programs. We believe that every child deserves a chance to learn and grow, and with your help, we can continue to make a difference in the lives of vulnerable children.

VISION

Our vision is to fill the gap for underserved and disadvantaged students of IDPs and refugee communities by supplying essential school items that facilitate their schooling and toward building a better society (whole society).

MISSION

Our mission is to provide school stationaries and scholarships to underserved students of internally displaced persons (IDPs) and refugee communities in South Sudan and other East African countries. In developing countries, wars disrupt youths' education, affecting future generations. This problem is even more daunting in a new country like South Sudan. The wars and internal conflicts disadvantage children of South Sudanese access to primary education. As wars forced communities to live in displaced and refugee camps for safety, children of South Sudan need/lack many items required for proper education, such as school supplies. In the developed world (e.g., the USA), school supplies like pencils, pens, and exercise books are taken for guaranteed.
