Current-partners: Pinydiit Foundation collaborates with several organizations in its efforts to improve education for young people in South Sudan. These partnerships provide the foundation with the necessary resources and expertise to achieve its mission of empowering underserved students through education. Through these collaborations, Pinydiit Foundation works closely with organizations that support refugees in the country and those that focus on education and community development in South Sudan. Together, these partnerships enable Pinydiit Foundation to identify and support students in need and provide them with school stationeries and scholarships.

Active Members:  Pinydiit Foundation's active members are a dedicated group of volunteers who are committed to the cause and share the belief that education is a fundamental right that should be accessible to all. The active members of the Pinydiit Foundation are composed of individuals from diverse backgrounds, including educators, community leaders, and individuals with a passion for social justice. They work tirelessly to identify and support students who face financial and social barriers to education. Through their efforts, the foundation is able to provide essential school supplies, such as exercise books, pens, pencils and erasers to students in need.

Milestone Achieved : While the organization is still in its early stages, it has already achieved two significant milestones: a) Its launch marks the beginning of its journey to empower young people through education. b) Founding of the organization- The organization was initiated by a dedicated team of individuals who share a passion for improving the lives of underserved students in the region. As the foundation continues to grow and expand its reach, it remains committed to providing access to education and supporting the academic pursuits of young people in need. c) We recently made our first impact by distributing 400+ books, pens,pencils and sharpeners, provided math sets to Class 8 students. Your support continues to make a difference

Awards : The organization is still in its early stages of development and has not yet received any awards for its work in the community. However, the foundation's commitment to its mission of providing school stationery and scholarships to underserved students of internally displaced persons (IDPs) and refugee communities of South Sudanese origin within South Sudan and other East African countries.
